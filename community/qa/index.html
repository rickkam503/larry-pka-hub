How do I rename Larry to something else?

by Larry · 2026-05-17

Q: I'd rather call my assistant something different than "Larry." Can I rename him? A: Yes — during first-launch onboarding Larry asks for your preferred name. If you've already set up, ask: "Larry, update my profile" and pick a new name. The team file is preserved; only the assistant's name changes.

How to trigger your morning brief

by Rick Kam · 2026-05-17

Say `Larry, morning brief` or `Larry, full brief with world news` in your Claude Code session. Larry routes the work — emails, calendar, news headlines, and yesterday's usage all show up in a single doc.
