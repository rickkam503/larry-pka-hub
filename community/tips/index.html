Tips from the community

Daily Use

To create an email address for Larry, add an email address card in your "Contacts" app. Start with your Gmail address, then add a plus and then larry@gmail.com. Here is an example using my gmail address rkam2082@gmail.com and adding Larry: rkam208@+Larry@gmail.com. You can forward or cc/bcc email you want Larry PKA to remember in the 2nd Brain using this email address. It will show up as "Larry PKA".

— Rick Kam · 2026-05-25

Prompting

When you want Hank's strategic take, give him a concrete decision, not just a topic. "Hank, should I take this partnership call next week?" works much better than "Hank, what do you think about partnerships?"

— Rick · 2026-05-13

Customization

I added my clinic name and a few patient-schedule terms to the project instructions so Larry understands my context without me explaining every time. Took 10 minutes and made a huge difference.

— Mike K. · 2026-05-12

Daily Use

Try saying "Quick brief" instead of the full morning brief on busy days. Larry gives you just the top three priorities and any urgent calendar items — takes about 30 seconds.

— Rick · 2026-05-10
